# BlockFin PME — Testing Instructions Guide

This guide is designed for non-technical users to test the different scenarios of the BlockFin PME application. Follow these steps sequentially to explore all platform features.

---

## 🛠️ Prerequisites

1. **MetaMask Extension**: Install the MetaMask extension in your browser (Chrome, Brave, or Edge).
2. **Local Blockchain (Ganache)**: Ensure the backend and a local blockchain (like Ganache) are running.
3. **Import Test Accounts**:
   - Open MetaMask.
   - Click on the account icon (top right) -> **Import Account**.
   - Paste the **Private Key** of the test accounts (provided by the dev team or found in `appsettings.Development.json`).
   - Repeat for Governor, PME, and Investor roles.

---

## 📂 Scenario 1: New User Registration (PME)

**Goal**: Register a new SME (PME) on the platform.

1. Open the application URL (usually `http://localhost:4200`).
2. Click on **Register** at the bottom of the sign-in card.
3. **Step 1: Connect Wallet**: Click **Connect with MetaMask**. Select the account you want to use as PME.
4. **Step 2: Select Role**: Choose **PME**.
5. **Step 3: Profile Details**:
   - **Company Name**: "Acme Innovations"
   - **Sector**: Select "Technology"
   - **Email**: "contact@acme.com"
6. Click **Submit Registration**.
7. **Sign the Message**: MetaMask will pop up. Click **Sign** to verify your identity.
8. You should see a success message: "Registration submitted, awaiting approval."

---

## 📂 Scenario 2: Governor Approval

**Goal**: As a platform administrator, approve the new PME registration.

1. Switch MetaMask to the **Governor** account.
2. Go to the **Login** page and click **Connect with MetaMask**.
3. Sign the message in MetaMask. You will be redirected to the **Governor Dashboard**.
4. Go to the **Registrations** tab.
5. Find "Acme Innovations" in the list.
6. Click the **Approve** button.
7. Confirm the action. The user status will change to "Approved".

---

## 📂 Scenario 3: PME Asset Management

**Goal**: Add and tokenize a company asset to use as collateral.

1. Switch MetaMask back to your **PME** account.
2. Login to the application. You will be redirected to the **PME Dashboard**.
3. Go to the **Assets** section (left menu).
4. Click **Add Asset**:
   - **Name**: "Server Rack Pro 2026"
   - **Type**: "Equipment"
   - **Value**: "5" (ETH)
   - **Description**: "High-performance server for cloud operations."
5. Click **Save Asset**. The asset appears in the list with status **REGISTERED**.
6. On the new asset, click the **Tokenize** button.
7. Confirm the transaction in MetaMask.
8. Wait a few seconds. The status should change to **ATO** (Asset Tokenized).

---

## 📂 Scenario 4: Financing Request

**Goal**: Request a loan using the tokenized asset as collateral.

1. In the **PME Dashboard**, go to the **Financing** section.
2. Click **Request Loan**.
3. Select the "Server Rack Pro 2026" from the list of available assets.
4. Enter the loan details:
   - **Amount**: "2" (ETH)
   - **Duration**: "30" (Days)
5. Click **Submit Request**.
6. Confirm the transaction in MetaMask.
7. The loan will now appear in the "My Loans" list with status **REQUESTED**.

---

## 📂 Scenario 5: Investor Funding

**Goal**: As an investor, fund the loan request.

1. Switch MetaMask to the **Investor** account.
2. Login to the application. You will be redirected to the **Investor Dashboard**.
3. Go to the **Marketplace**.
4. Find the loan for "Acme Innovations" with the amount of 2 ETH.
5. Click the **Fund** button.
6. Confirm the payment of 2 ETH in MetaMask.
7. The loan status will change to **FUNDED**.

---

## 📂 Scenario 6: Loan Repayment

**Goal**: Repay the loan as the PME.

1. Switch MetaMask back to the **PME** account.
2. Login and go to the **Financing** section.
3. Find your funded loan.
4. Click the **Repay** button.
5. Confirm the repayment (2 ETH + interest) in MetaMask.
6. The loan status will change to **REPAID**, and your collateral asset will be released (status back to **ATO**).

---

## 📂 Scenario 7: Guarantor Participation

**Goal**: As a guarantor, provide an asset to the platform.

1. Switch MetaMask to the **Guarantor** account.
2. Login to the application. You will be redirected to the **Guarantor Dashboard**.
3. Click **Add Asset** (or go to **My Guarantees**).
4. Fill in the asset details:
   - **Asset Name**: "Guarantee Fund A"
   - **Type**: "Other"
   - **Value**: "10" (ETH)
   - **Organization**: "SafeGuard TN"
5. Click **Submit**.
6. The asset will appear in your dashboard, ready to be used to back PME loans if needed.

---

## 💡 Troubleshooting Tips

- **Transaction Pending**: If a transaction takes too long, check MetaMask. You might need to "Speed Up" or ensure you have enough test ETH.
- **Wrong Role**: If you don't see the right dashboard, check which account is active in MetaMask and re-login.
- **Approval Needed**: If you can't access PME features after registration, ensure the Governor has approved your wallet.
